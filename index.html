4 Strategic Questions for Thomas

These questions will shape VSL's Morocco GTM strategy. Your VC experience is critical here.

Q1: Licensing & Partnership Strategy
How should we structure partnerships with Moroccan teams?
Moroccan football clubs likely lack mature merchandise programs (unlike European clubs). Should VSL: (a) pursue formal licensing agreements with individual clubs, (b) build a direct B2C merchandise model (no licensing required), or (c) position ourselves as the infrastructure provider helping teams build merchandise capabilities?
Q2: Market Development Without Catalysts
How do we build sustainable demand in an emerging market?
Unlike developed markets, Morocco has no major external catalyst. Should we focus on organic partnership revenue from clubs or invest in B2C fan engagement? What's the typical timeline to profitability? Which seasons/tournaments should we target (African Cup of Nations, local leagues)?
Q3: Capital & Unit Economics
What should our revenue model look like?
Given Morocco's $47M market size and lower purchasing power vs. developed markets: What's a realistic TAM we can capture? Should we bootstrap with print-on-demand/digital merch first, or invest in physical inventory? At what revenue threshold do club partnerships become sustainable? What capital runway should VSL plan?
Q4: Team Partnership Sequencing
How should we prioritize partner outreach?
We have ~8-10 major football clubs + basketball opportunities. Should we secure one flagship partnership first as proof-of-concept, or pursue multi-team approach from day one? What does your experience show about concentration vs. diversification in emerging markets?
